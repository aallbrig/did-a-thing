User Manual

How to Use the App

Toggle the Daily Button

On the home page, you'll see a large circular button with "I did it!" text. Click this button to mark today as complete. When clicked, the button will display a beautiful animated rainbow border to celebrate your accomplishment!

View Your Progress

Below the button, you'll find a calendar view showing your tracking history. Days when you completed your task are marked with green dots. You can navigate between months using the arrow buttons.

Understanding the Calendar
  • Green dots: Days when you successfully did the thing
  • No dot: Days when you didn't complete your task
  • Today: Highlighted with a blue border
Data Storage

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. This means your data persists between sessions, but it's only available on the device and browser you're using.

About

This is a real-time tracking application to help ensure you do a daily thing each day.

Whether it's drinking enough water, taking a walk, practicing gratitude, or any other daily habit you want to build, "I Did a Thing!" helps you stay consistent and visualize your progress over time.

Pro Tips:
  • Set a specific daily goal to track
  • Check the app at the same time each day
  • Celebrate your streaks and don't be too hard on yourself for missed days
  • The app works offline once loaded, so you can track anywhere!
